🗺️ Interactive Day-by-Day Route Maps

01 Fri

Day 1 — The Charm of Gamla Stan

Arrival & check-in, lunch at Fuori di Pizza, vinyl records at Sound Pollution (must visit before 18:00!), and a scenic evening walking loop through Stortorget and Gamla Stan alleys, ending with dinner at Stockholms Gästabud.

🗺️ Map 📄 Travel Card 📖 Nobel Museum Guide
02 Sat

Day 2 — Royal History, Art & Södermalm Views

Morning Royal Palace guided tour (10:30) + the Royal Armoury (Erik XIV's parade armour & the horse Streiff), lunch/visit at Nationalmuseum (Old Masters), fika at Café Blom on Skeppsholmen, and a spectacular sunset walk along Södermalm's high-cliffs (Skinnarviksberget & Monteliusvägen).

🗺️ Map 📄 Travel Card 📖 Royal Palace Guide 📖 Royal Armoury Guide 📖 Nationalmuseum Guide 📖 Moderna Museet Guide
03 Sun

Day 3 — Maritime History & A Perfect Circle

⚠️ Sunday breakfast opens 08:00 — quick bite, then depart Odenplan 08:15. Early Vasa Museum (beat the crowds), Strandvägen promenade, Hallwyl Museum (self-guided — the English tour is Saturdays only), a stroll through Kungsträdgården & its cave-art metro station (moved from Day 2), lunch at Vapiano (Santorini is closed Sun lunch), hotel rest, then ferry over to Gröna Lund for the A Perfect Circle concert! Tram 7 home — the ferry stops early evening.

🗺️ Map 📄 Travel Card 📖 Vasa Museum Guide 📖 Hallwyl Museum Guide
04 Mon

Day 4 — City Hall, KTH, Vinyl & SOAD Concert

City Hall Tower climb first (09:50 — booked ✅), then the City Hall building tour (11:00 — booked ✅), visit to KTH Royal Institute campus, vinyl hunt at Nostalgipalatset, Mediterranean lunch at Cravings (Mykonos is closed Mondays), hotel rest, then to Strawberry Arena in Solna for System of a Down — doors 18:30, with Queens of the Stone Age & Acid Bath on the bill!

🗺️ Map 📄 Travel Card 📖 City Hall Guide
05 Tue

Day 5 — Skansen, Djurgården & Gamla Stan by Night

Gentle post-concert morning at Skansen Open-Air Museum, coastal walk to Waldemarsudde gallery, fika at Café Ektorpet, fire-grilled dinner at Eld, then a ferry back for a golden-hour stroll through Gamla Stan (Stortorget and the lamplit Old Town alleys), a booked last-evening dinner at Tradition (19:45), and a final lamplit Gamla Stan farewell stroll under the late-June sky.

🗺️ Map 📄 Travel Card 📖 Skansen Guide 📖 Waldemarsudde Guide
06 Wed

Day 6 — Last Swedish Morning & Departure

Breakfast at the hotel, then coffee & a sweet at Pom & Flora (Odengatan 39), grab buns at Fabrique, stroll Observatorielunden hilltop, check-out, and board the Pendeltåg commuter rail to Arlanda Airport for the flight home.
